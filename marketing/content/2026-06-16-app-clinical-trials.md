What if eligibility-screening tooling for a clinical trial didn't just get generated once — but kept improving until it matched real protocol criteria?

That's a loop. Point it at the trial's inclusion/exclusion rules, let an **independent referee** grade the tool against real, captured criteria (never the model grading itself), refactor the weakest dimension, and lock every fix in as a regression test. An unsafe screening rule never ships — safety is a terminal gate, not a score you can outrun.

**Clinical trials** are one of 10+ applications on the **loop-engineering-anything** roadmap. The loop engine is built and proven; the domain loops are open invitations.

High-stakes domains are exactly where "improve until a referee says it's right" beats "generate once and hope."

If you work in trials — or just believe this should exist — come build the loop with us. One PR adds the recipe.
